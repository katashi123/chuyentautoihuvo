==========The Midnight Train to Nowhere==========

Title	 	The Midnight Train to Nowhere
Version		1.0
Tool		RPG Maker MV
Creator		Mannytsu
Genre		Adventure

==========About==========

George boards the last train of the day. It leaves at midnight, and the destination: Nowhere...

The game was created for Indie Game Maker Contest 2017 in a span of one month.

==========Controls==========

Arrow keys		Move
Z/Space/Enter		Select, Investigate
X/Esc			Menu, Cancel
Hold Shift		Run
